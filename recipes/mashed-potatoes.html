Mashed Potatoes

Description

"These mashed potatoes with heavy cream are too fattening to have on a regular basis. I only make these on holidays, and everyone wonders what's in them that's makes them so rich."

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • ½ cup of butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place potatoes into a large pot with enough water to cover; bring to a boil. Cook until fork tender, about 20 minutes. Drain.
  3. Add butter to potatoes in the pot. Mash using a potato masher, ricer, or mixer until smooth. Gradually beat in heavy cream until well combined. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
